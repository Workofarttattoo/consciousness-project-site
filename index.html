Initiative

Open Consciousness Atlas

The Atlas aggregates theories, experiments, and methodological notes across neuroscience, philosophy of mind, contemplative science, and speculative design. Each entry is peer reviewed by two community editors and cross-referenced with related work.

  • Format: Markdown entries synced to an open repository.
  • How to contribute: Submit a pull request or share an outline via the submission form.
  • Maintainers: Consciousness Lab, Phenomenology Guild, and invited guest editors.
