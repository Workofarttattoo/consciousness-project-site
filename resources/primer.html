Consciousness Primer

A five-week syllabus guiding interdisciplinary teams through foundational theories, research paradigms, and facilitated first-person practice.

Download facilitator kit Back to overview

Syllabus outline

  1. Week 1: Definitions, history, and the hard problem.
  2. Week 2: Neural correlates, global workspace, and empirical paradigms.
  3. Week 3: Neurophenomenology and contemplative methods.
  4. Week 4: Integrated information, predictive processing, and complexity.
  5. Week 5: Futures, ethics, and community salon facilitation.

Each session includes a 60-minute discussion guide, annotated reading list, and exercises for first-person reporting. The toolkit comes with editable slides, journal prompt cards, and observation log templates.

Recommended use

  • Onboard new research collaborators or studio residents in under six weeks.
  • Ground speculative design or AI safety teams in cross-disciplinary vocabulary.
  • Host public study groups by pairing readings with community salons.

Want a facilitator from the Consciousness Project to host a session with your team? Email hello@consciousness.project with your objectives.
